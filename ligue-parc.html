CASABLANCA

Parc de la Ligue Arabe

The largest green space in Casablanca, Parc de la Ligue Arabe is a peaceful oasis in the heart of the city. Originally designed in 1913 by French architect Albert Laprade, the park features palm-lined avenues, French garden layouts, and restored historic arches. After its renovation in 2019, it now offers modern playgrounds, open lawns, walking paths, and art installations. It's a favorite for locals and tourists alike to relax, jog, or enjoy a sunny afternoon.
